Welcome

Basketball has always been more than a game — it’s a laboratory of strategy, athleticism, and storytelling. Classroom to Court bridges the gap between the academic study of sport and the hardwood itself, turning raw statistics into narratives that every fan can appreciate.

Whether you follow the high-stakes drama of March Madness or track every NBA standings shift across an 82-game season, this site gives you something most coverage doesn’t: the numbers behind the moments. Every chart, every breakdown, and every post here is built from real game-by-game data — no filler, no recycled takes, just basketball and the statistics that explain it.
